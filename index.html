Kavita Ingale


"Give me six hours to chop down a tree and I will spend the first four sharpening the axe"

Abraham Lincoln



After finishing her post graduation in Economics from University of Mumbai, Kavita started her career with Sydenham College of Commerece & Economics as a lecturer. It was then that she realised that research is a building block of an academic career. This motivated her to join Economic and Political Weekly Foundation a research assistant. There she worked on various research projects like Money Market, ICICI-EPWRF Database projects, etc.


Always enticed by dynamics of financial markets, she grabbed the opportunity at Bonanza Portfolio Ltd. as a Research Analyst. There she had hands on work experience in the area of equity research and portfolio management.


After gaining a few years of corporate experience, her fervour for teaching directed her back to the field of education. She has taught various subjects in Economics and Fnance in renowned B-Schools in Pune, viz: Sinhgad Institute of Management, MIT School of Business and MIT School of Management. Presently she is pursuing Ph.D. in the area of Household Finance with the objective of knowledge advancement to bridge the gap between theory and practice.


She firmly believes that, "Economics preaches the philosophy of life - constrained optimism, while Finance teaches how to practice it."
